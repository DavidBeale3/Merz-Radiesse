Dr Gabriela Casabona
Dr Gabriela Casabona has decades of experience in aesthetic medicine, with expertize in both surgical and non-surgical treatments. She had her own aesthetic practice in her home country of Brazil for 18 years and is now the Medical Director for the aesthetics and skin cancer departments at Ocean Clinic, Marbella, where dermal fillers remain a core part of her work. Dr Casabona also holds the title of Director of Clinical Research, a testament to her many published papers on the topics of combined treatment techniques and anti-aging procedures. She has collaborated with respected institutions, such as the Universidade ABC-Brazil, and has been working with Merz Aesthetics since 2008.
